Transparent Donations, Powered by Blockchain

NGOTrust is a plug-and-play payment gateway that enables transparent donations to NGOs worldwide. Track every dollar, prevent misuse, and empower charitable organizations.

About NGOTrust

Our mission and vision

NGOTrust was born out of a simple idea: charitable donations should be transparent, secure, and accountable. We're a team of blockchain enthusiasts, developers, and social impact advocates committed to transforming how charitable giving works.

By leveraging blockchain technology and artificial intelligence, we've created a platform that ensures every donation reaches its intended purpose, while providing complete transparency to donors and operational efficiency to NGOs.
